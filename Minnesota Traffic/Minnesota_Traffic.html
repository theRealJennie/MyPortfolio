Minnesota Traffic Project

This was a project from my Google Business Intelligence class. For this project I used Tableau to analyze interstate highway traffic in Minnesota over a period of time.

I was provided the traffic data (.csv linked below) and given a message from my supervisor explaining that the Minnesota Department of Transportation wanted to find out traffic volume trends on the interstate highways to inform possible infrastructure changes.
